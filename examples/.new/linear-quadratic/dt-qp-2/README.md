## dt-qp-2

### Formulation
```math
\begin{align*}
\text{changing :} \quad & u, x \\
\text{minimize :} \quad & m [x(t_{f})]^{2} + \int_{0}^{t_{f}}r u^{2} \mathrm{d}t \\
\text{subject to :} \quad & \dot{x} = ax + b \sin(\omega t) u \\
& x(0) = x_{0}\\
\end{align*}
```

### Solution
A closed-form solution is available for this problem.
