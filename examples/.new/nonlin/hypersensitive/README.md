## hypersensitive

### Formulation
```math
\begin{align*}
\text{changing :} \quad & u,x \\
\text{minimize :} \quad & \int_{0}^{t_f} \left[ x^2 + u^2 \right] \mathrm{d}t \\
\text{subject to :} \quad & \dot{x} = -x^3 + u \\
& x(0) = 1.5 \\
& x(t_f) = 1
\end{align*}
```
