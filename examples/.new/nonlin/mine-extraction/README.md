## mine-extraction

### Formulation
```math
\begin{align*}
\text{changing :} \quad & u,x \\
\text{minimize :} \quad & \int_{0}^{t_f} \left[ \frac{u^2(t)}{x(t)} - a u(t) \right] \mathrm{d}t \\
\text{subject to :} \quad & \dot{x} = - u(t) \\
& x(0) = x_0 \\
& 0 \leq x(t) \leq x_0
\end{align*}
```
